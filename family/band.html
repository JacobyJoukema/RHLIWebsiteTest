The RHLI Band

The Regimental Band of the Royal Hamilton Light Infantry (RHLI) is an operational sub-unit within the regiment whose role is to provide musical support to the RHLI in order to foster high morale and esprit de corps, to participate in programs designed to enhance community relations, to make Canadians aware of the Canadian Armed Forces and to instill national pride.
