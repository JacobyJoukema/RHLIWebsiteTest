RHLI Veterans Association

The Object of the Association is to perpetuate the memory of the members of the First Battalion, Royal Hamilton Light Infantry (Wentworth Regiment), and bring about the unity of those who have served therein and such others who may be allied, associated or connected with it, or sympathetic to the aims and objectives of the Association and to promote the welfare of the members thereof as more particularly set out in the Letters Patent.

The Association is located at 1353 Barton Street east. Hamilton Ontario Phone 905-545-4611 Email - rhliveterans@cogeco.net
