Regimental Events

The RHLI works hard but also knows how to play hard too. Throughout the year the regiment organizes and runs events ranging from traditional dinners to hockey games between infantry regiments. Many events are extended to the families of serving members giving them the opportunity to experience some military culture, tradition and comradery.

A World of Opportunities

Hockey

The Nations Passtime

Members of the RHLI can lace up and join the hockey team where they play against local teams and other infantry units.

Regimental Dinners

Serving Up Tradition

Serving members, family as well as associates of the regiment have to opportunity to attend regimental dinners throughout the year celebrating current and past accomplishments of the RHLI.
